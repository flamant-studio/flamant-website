The About section

Hey —
here I am.

52 years old, married to Stine, three kids, one dog, one cat. Living in Brønshøj, Copenhagen.

In both my professional and personal life I try to live by principles suchs as strength, integrity, compassion and quality. I particularly enjoy working on website design and ongoing digital management — I find real satisfaction in clear usability, great communication and results you can actually measure. I enjoy making pretty things that matter.

Lately I've been deep in AI — and I've found genuine new inspiration through it. I'm currently running on Claude as my primary tool and working on a number of AI-powered projects, including building my own product studio from scratch. At Organic Basics I leverage Claude for project management and ecommerce more specifically.

What I do

Digital growth.
(and translation)

🛍️

Ecommerce & Digital Growth

Twenty years in digital commerce — running teams, owning roadmaps, caring obsessively about conversion. Currently Head of Ecommerce at Organic Basics. Previously Global Digital Director at Trustpilot and Kahoot!. I've done the hard miles.

🏗️

Product Development

Currently building Personal Shopper — a taste-driven shopping board for Europe with no ads, ever. I design, spec and ship from scratch, with AI agents doing the heavy lifting. This is what evenings and weekends look like when you're slightly obsessed.

🌐

Organisation & Consultancy

Helping leadership and digital teams get the fundamentals right — strategy, structure, prioritisation, roadmapping. I've worked across telco, audio, SaaS and fashion at director level. Sometimes the most valuable thing is a fresh pair of eyes and someone who'll tell you what's actually going on.

📖

Translation

French → Danish translation for Forlaget Zoom since 2014. Over 100 titles and counting — Franco-Belgian bandes dessinées ranging from detective noir to sci-fi to old-school adventure. Being half-French with a cand.mag. in Danish turns out to be a fairly specific skill set.

The original flamant.dk. Written in the third person. Tracked monthly statistics including cola intake (13.5 litres), toilet seat incidents (149.2/month) and satisfaction rating (78%). Navigation via five icon categories. Absolutely unhinged.
